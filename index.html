Inside of Mental Injustice

A voice for the wrongly incarcerated — and a platform for people impacted by incarceration and mental illness.

Our Mission

Transforming lived experience into media that inspires accountability.

IMI exists to tell the stories the system tried to bury—stories that expose psychological harm, institutional neglect, and systemic injustice. We produce survivor-led media that is commercially viable, culturally necessary, and strategically designed to move policy, shift public opinion, and protect human dignity.
